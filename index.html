Countries With The Refusal Rates for U.S. Visa [2006-2016]

What is it?

It is a visualization of Annual US Visa Rejection rate for different countries around the world. You can find more infohere.

How to?

Select at least one country from the picker and click on Draw Map to view the country. You can add up to 7 for comparison. Clicking on a country will show the bar plot of it's past year visa rejection rates.

Troubleshooting

If nothing happens on clicking a country on the map, use FireFox or if you're using Chrome, restart it to enjoy the visualization properly.
